# Coaching and Delegation

Use these rules when growing engineers without sacrificing execution quality.

## Delegation Rules

- Delegate complete problems with context, constraints, and decision boundaries.
- State what must be escalated versus what can be decided locally.
- Increase ownership gradually with evidence of judgment and follow-through.

## Coaching Rules

- Ask questions first when the engineer is close to the answer.
- Be more directive when risk is high, time is short, or the gap is too large.
- Review not only what was built, but how decisions were made.

## Team Growth Rules

- Keep the thought process visible through reviews, docs, and walkthroughs.
- Reward clarity, ownership, and collaboration, not only speed.
- Build successors and backups for every critical responsibility.
