# Discovery Evidence Bundle

Every discovery cycle should be able to hand off a compact evidence bundle.

## Minimum bundle

1. One-page discovery brief
2. Contextual use scenario
3. Low-fidelity prototype or flow sketch
4. Validation notes
5. Requirements derived from findings
6. Go / refine / drop recommendation

## Acceptance test

A delivery team should be able to read the bundle and answer:

- Who is the user in this moment?
- What job are they trying to complete?
- What did we validate?
- What remains uncertain?
- Why is the recommended next step justified?

If not, the discovery is not done.
